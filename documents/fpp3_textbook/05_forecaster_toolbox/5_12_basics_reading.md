## 5.12Further reading

- Ord et al. (2017)provides further discussion of simple benchmark forecasting methods.
- A review of forecast evaluation methods is given inHyndman & Koehler (2006), looking at the strengths and weaknesses of different approaches. This is the paper that introduced the MASE as a general-purpose forecast accuracy measure.
- For a discussion of forecasting using STL, seeTheodosiou (2011).
- An excellent discussion of evaluating distributional forecast accuracy is provided byGneiting & Katzfuss (2014).
